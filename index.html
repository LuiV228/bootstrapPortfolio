From the very beginning of my career aspirations, I was resolute about becoming an actuary. I was drawn to the world of probabilities, risk management, and financial forecasting. The idea of using complex mathematical models to make sense of uncertainty in the business world held a deep fascination for me. This initial ambition fueled my academic and professional journey, laying the foundation for my career in the actuarial field, where I continued to use my quantitative skills to analyze and mitigate risk.

Although my initial passion was firmly rooted in becoming an actuary, my interests evolved over time. I became increasingly captivated by the intricate world of cryptography, with its emphasis on data security and encryption. This shift in my aspirations was driven by a growing curiosity for the art of code-breaking and protecting sensitive information. Ultimately, I made the decision to transition my career path towards becoming a cryptographer, where I could apply my analytical skills to the fascinating realm of cyber security.

After some reflection and exploration of my career options, I found myself drawn towards the dynamic field of data science. While I initially considered a path as a cryptographer, the potential to harness the power of data and analytics in diverse industries deeply intrigued me. The versatility of data science and its ability to uncover valuable insights from vast datasets felt like the perfect fit for my evolving interests. I decided to pivot my career trajectory towards becoming a data scientist, where I could apply my analytical skills and passion for problem-solving to address a wide range of real-world challenges through data-driven solutions.
